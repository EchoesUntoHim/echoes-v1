# v1.15.26 상세 수정 보고서 (Detailed Diff)

## 1. 개요
가사 생성 시 단어 중간 끊김(Character breaking) 현상을 해결하고, 한-영 1:1 행 수 매칭을 엄격하게 적용하기 위한 긴급 수정을 완료하였습니다.

## 2. 파일별 수정 내역

### 가. src/prompts/Lyrics.txt
```diff
-- 1:1 SYNC: Korean and English must have identical line counts and matching meanings line by line.
+- 1:1 SYNC: Korean and English must have EXACTLY identical line counts. Every single line in Korean must have a corresponding, meaning-matched line in English at the same position.
...
+- NO WORD BREAKS: NEVER break a single word into two lines. Always break lines at word boundaries (spaces) or phrase boundaries.
+- SEMANTIC BREAKS: Break lines naturally based on musical rhythm and meaning, not by character count.
```

### 나. src/hooks/useLyricsLogic.ts
```diff
-        - 1:1 LINE SYNC: Maintain a 1:1 match between Korean and English lines.
-        - NATURAL FLOW: Write naturally and expressively. Avoid being stiff or uniform in line length.
+        - 1:1 LINE SYNC: Every line in Korean must have an exactly corresponding line in English. Line counts MUST match perfectly.
+        - NATURAL FLOW: Write naturally. NEVER break a word in the middle. Always break lines at semantic or word boundaries.
```

## 3. 검증 포인트
- **단어 보존**: 한글 단어(예: 맞춰, 덮어주는)가 줄바꿈에 의해 쪼개지지 않는지 확인.
- **의미 단위 줄바꿈**: 기계적인 글자 수가 아닌, 가창 호흡에 맞는 자연스러운 문장 단위 줄바꿈 확인.
- **1:1 동기화**: 한국어 가사 한 줄당 영어 가사 한 줄이 정확히 대응되는지 확인.
